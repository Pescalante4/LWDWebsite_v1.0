Who We Are

Landscape Wholesale Distributors (LWD) is a family-owned supplier with decades of experience serving the Southwest. Our mission is simple: deliver premium plants on time, with unmatched consistency and care. We support landscapers, developers, and contractors with an inventory they can count on — and a team they can trust.
